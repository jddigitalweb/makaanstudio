About Us.

Makaan Studio is an interior and architecture consultancy practice working across residential, commercial, hospitality, and styling projects across multiple cities. Rooted in warmth, calmness, and timeless design, the studio believes every space should feel deeply personal to the people experiencing it. Rather than following a fixed design style, each project evolves through its context, emotions, and the lifestyle of its users — balancing functionality with softness, materiality, and detail.

Our Philosophy

"Luxury, to us, is not excess.
